Driving Licence Controlled Smart Vehicle

Duration: Jun 2022 – Jun 2024

Repo: github.com/tirth2812/driving_licence_controlled_smart_vehicle

Overview

Advanced driver verification system that authenticates the driver before enabling vehicle ignition. Integrated RFID, fingerprint, and facial recognition technologies to ensure only licensed users can operate the vehicle.

Authentication Workflow

  • Prompt for validation on unlock → scan RFID or fingerprint
  • ✅ On success: ignition enabled and system switches to media player UI.
  • ❌ On failure: ignition remains disabled and alert mechanism is triggered.

Hardware

  • Hardware: Raspberry Pi, RC522/MFRC522 RFID reader, R305 fingerprint sensor, LED indicators, media player interface.

My Contributions

  • Assembled and wired the entire hardware system, planning module-to-controller connections and ensuring reliable signal integrity.
  • Mapped and verified all communication protocols (SPI for RFID, UART for fingerprint/GSM/GPS, I²C for LCD) and tested each device individually before final integration.
  • Coordinated dashboard-level hardware fitting, prepared wiring layout, and validated communication across the system.
